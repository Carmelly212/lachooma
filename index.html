About La Chooma

La Chooma, Alon Carmelly's Tribal Latin Band, plays uncompromising Afro-Latin grooves while blending sounds from the realms of psychedelia, deep jungles, and the North African desert. Gentle melodies, warm synthesizers, and a comforting desert vibe create a colorful and unique sound that effortlessly gets you dancing away.

They are preparing for their second single with the London-based label Batov Records and the release of their debut album in the summer of 2025. Alon Carmelly, formerly the double bass player for 'The Apples', together with the band members — all of whom are active and well-known musicians in Israel's groove scene — are now on tour.
