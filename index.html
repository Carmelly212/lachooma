About La Chooma

La Chooma, Alon Carmelly's Tribal Latin Band, plays uncompromising Afro-Latin grooves while blending sounds from the realms of psychedelia, deep jungles, and the North African desert.

La Chooma’s self-titled debut for Batov Records. Drawing on Moroccan Gnawa, Colombian cumbia, Jamaica dub & roots, and cosmic jazz, the six-piece ensemble create deep, hypnotic music rooted in global traditions and shaped for contemporary dancefloors.
